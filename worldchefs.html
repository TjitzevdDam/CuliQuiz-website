Where we start

The same mission, from two sides

Worldchefs has built something rare. A network of chefs across 110 countries, more than two hundred national associations, and an Academy that has welcomed tens of thousands of aspiring chefs in eight languages. Few associations carry this much reach and this much mission at the same time.

We want the same thing you do. To keep this a craft, not a stopgap job, for the whole next generation. Worldchefs brings a chef into the profession. We keep the craft alive in them, every day after.

The idea

A daily layer on top of your Academy

The Academy gets a chef their foundation and their first job. What keeps it alive after is a daily habit. CuliQuiz is that habit: three to five minutes a day, gamified, on the phone, the way this generation actually learns. Wine, ingredients, technique, the story on the plate.

The plan

How we'd build it, step by step

Nothing big to sign. A phased path that proves itself before it grows, at the pace your governance allows.

Step 1

A pilot

One association, one country. A branded CuliQuiz layer on top of the Academy for a first group of chefs. We build it, you open the door.

Where it proves itself
Step 2

Proof

We measure engagement and reach, and turn it into a shared case study you can show your members and partners.

Step 3

Rollout

Open it to more member associations, in more languages, at the pace that fits your governance and budget cycle.

Step 4

A standard

The daily craft layer becomes part of what Worldchefs membership means, recognised across the network.

The partnership

A door, both ways

Simple and mutual. You open your network to us, we open our platform to you. Neither side has to build anything new.

What CuliQuiz brings Worldchefs

  • A daily interaction and education layer on top of your Academy
  • A rejuvenating step that reaches the next generation the way they learn
  • Access to the app for your members and associations
  • At no cost, and we build it, not you

What we'd ask of Worldchefs

  • The Worldchefs name on our platform, as an official partner
  • Visibility inside your Academy, your platform and your website
  • Access to your network of countries and associations

In one line: your members get the app, we get your platform. You gain access to us, we gain access to you. Nobody builds a second thing, and we strengthen each other.

We're not asking for an agreement.
