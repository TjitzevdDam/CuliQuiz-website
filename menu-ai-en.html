User story

Why we are building this

Building a training is work: thinking up questions, checking answers, assembling content. That is exactly the barrier that holds new customers back. Menu AI removes that barrier.

As a hospitality operator I want to upload my menu, so my team trains on our own dishes within moments, without me having to create questions by hand.
The core promise: take the work away. From upload to a playable training in moments, not days.
Manager / trainer

… wants to choose how the training is offered (fixed campaign, tournament or battle), so adoption is fast.

CuliQuiz (platform)

… wants every upload to add validated content to the database, so we build the largest hospitality knowledge base.

The user flow

One upload, five steps

From document to playable training. Step 1 is everything the user does themselves, the rest happens automatically.

1

Upload

The operator uploads the food or drinks menu (PDF or photo). Phase 2: SOP documents.

2

Recognise

Menu AI extracts dishes, ingredients and drinks. Plus price and composition per dish.

3

Store

Everything lands structured in the database: price, composition, allergens, linked to the knowledge taxonomy.

4

Turn into content

The input is turned into games and quizzes, validated, in our own game types.

5

Offer

The user picks the format: fixed campaign, tournament or battle for fast adoption.

What Menu AI recognises

From menu to building blocks

Menu AI reads the menu and pulls out the individual elements. Those elements are the basis for all content.

Dish

Carpaccio, coq au vin, nasi, satay

Ingredient

Beef tenderloin, chicken, broccoli, hollandaise, truffle mayonnaise

Drink

Wine, coffee, teas

SOP

Brand standards, HACCP guidelines, cleaning schedules
Phase 2
What we store

Every menu becomes structured data

The extraction is not only for that one venue’s training. It is stored, reused and builds the largest content database in the sector.

Price of a dish

Per item, for later analysis and upselling content.

Composition of the dish

The ingredients and preparation behind each dish.

Allergens

Derived from the composition, per dish.

Knowledge mapping

Linked to domain, subdomain and topic from the Knowledge Graph.

The strategic core

A mega content database

It is not just that one venue training. Every uploaded menu enriches the global content pool: more dishes, more ingredients, more validated questions. The more menus, the stronger the platform for everyone.

1 upload= content for the whole database
From input to content

Validated content, in our data model

The output is not loose JSON to look at, but exactly the format the platform ingests: a Content Set of questions, linked to the Knowledge Graph and our game types. So the tooling can use it right away.

Input

Document

Food menu, drinks menu (PDF/photo).

AI

Extraction

Dishes, ingredients, drinks, price, composition.

Human-in-the-loop

Validation

Checked by us before it goes live. Quality guaranteed.

Output

Content Set

source_type: organization, questions with domain/topic + game type.

Tooling

Distribution

Campaign, tournament or battle.

Distribution

The user picks the format

The same content, three ways to roll it out. The operator picks what fits the goal.

Fixed campaign

A continuous training programme, with a fixed duration and goals. For structural knowledge building.

Tournament

A temporary competition with a leaderboard. For energy and a shared peak moment.

Battle

Fast, head-to-head. For instant adoption: play right away, learn right away.

Phasing

First the menu, then the SOPs

We start with the most direct win, and expand once that is in place.

Phase 1 · first

Food & drinks menu

  • Upload of food and drinks menu
  • Recognition: dishes, ingredients, drinks
  • Storage: price, composition, allergens
  • Validated games and quizzes
  • Distribution: campaign, tournament, battle
Phase 2 · later

SOPs

  • Brand standards
  • HACCP guidelines
  • Cleaning schedules
  • Compliance and procedure training from your own documents
Expectations for the tech team

What it must meet

Acceptance criteria, so we share the same picture of “done”.

Take the work away, within moments

From upload to playable training with minimal actions. Speed is the core promise.

Output conforms to the Knowledge Architecture

Every question linked to domain / subdomain / topic + source_type, in a game type from the platform.

Validated content

A validation step (human-in-the-loop) before content goes live. No unchecked AI output to players.

Importable by the tooling

The Content Set is directly selectable in the campaign builder. No intermediate steps or manual rework.

Feeds the central database

All extracted elements enrich the global content pool, not just your own organisation.

Choice of distribution

When offering it, the user chooses between campaign, tournament or battle.
