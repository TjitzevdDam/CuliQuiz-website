The insight

The one thing no POS can measure

Every restaurant on Lightspeed can see what sold, when, and for how much. What no system shows is why. Did the guest order the better wine because someone knew how to recommend it? Did the upsell happen because the team actually understood the menu?

Training is the one lever every operator pulls and nobody can measure. It stays a cost on a spreadsheet, never a number you can prove. Until you connect it to the till.

What CuliQuiz brings

A daily training layer, not another course

CuliQuiz is a daily game of three to five minutes that keeps craft alive in hospitality teams. Wine, menu knowledge, upsell, cross-sell, allergens, the story behind what is on the plate. What Duolingo did for language, we do for the floor.

Around fifty thousand users across the Netherlands, partnerships with brands like Coca-Cola, Hendi and Rational, and a platform rebuilt to scale globally from next year. It already runs for internal teams, partner trainings, and external onboarding. The same engine that trains a team can train a merchant base.
