New! CuliQuiz Live.

As of today, CuliQuiz Live is here — the newest addition to the CuliQuiz family. A live quiz platform that lets you battle an entire class on culinary knowledge in one go.

What is CuliQuiz Live?

CuliQuiz Live is the live quiz for the classroom or your company event. The teacher (or host) projects the questions on the big screen, players play along on their own phone or laptop. Scores appear in real time on screen — who answered the most correctly, who grabs the fastest points, and who wins the battle?

How does it work?

  1. Create a free account at culiquiz-live.nl.
  2. Pick a quiz from the library or create your own questions.
  3. Share the PIN with your class or team. Everyone joins — within 10 seconds the whole group is in.
  4. Play. Educational, loud and extremely fanatical.

For who?

For anyone who wants to test knowledge with a group. From teachers at vocational and hotel schools, to restaurant managers who want to train their team in a playful way, to companies closing their event with a battle, or simply friends wanting a fun evening around food and drink.

Why we’re building this

Because fun learning sticks. Daily microlearning in the CuliQuiz app plus that one incredibly fun live battle session in the classroom — that’s the combination that grows craftsmanship. Knowledge when it counts, wrapped in a game everyone looks forward to.
