Mocca d’Or new partner of CuliQuiz

With pride we welcome Coffee Roastery Mocca d’Or as a new partner of CuliQuiz.

As a coffee specialist with years of experience in the hospitality sector, Mocca d’Or stands for craftsmanship, quality and knowledge — exactly the elements we want to strengthen within CuliQuiz.

Together we’ll build a strong coffee campaign centred on knowledge, experience and interaction. From origin and branding to extraction and guest experience, with Mocca d’Or as the Netherlands’ coffee expert.

We’re looking forward to a great collaboration in which we’ll inspire and develop the next generation of hospitality professionals together.

Welcome on board!
