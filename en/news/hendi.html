CuliQuiz and HENDI start a three-year partnership

CuliQuiz and HENDI — Tools for Chefs have started a three-year partnership. A collaboration where craftsmanship, technique and knowledge come together.

HENDI is visible every day in professional kitchens. With CuliQuiz we’re adding an extra layer to that: knowledge activation. Not just showing what you use, but also understanding why and how to use it best.

Over the coming years we’re building together on:

  • Educational activations around technique
  • Interactive challenges for (young) professionals in the CuliQuiz app
  • Live moments where innovation and craftsmanship come together
  • Structural visibility within the hospitality community

Not a one-off campaign. But a long-term commitment to the next generation of professionals.
