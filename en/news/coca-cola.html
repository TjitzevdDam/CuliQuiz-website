Coca-Cola and CuliQuiz are teaming up

From January, we’re starting our collaboration with Coca-Cola Europacific Partners Netherlands.

Together we’re focusing on activating hospitality knowledge on the work floor. Short, playful and fitting the daily rhythm of hospitality. No classical training, but learning by doing.

CuliQuiz fits well with the vision of Coca-Cola Europacific Partners. As Anouk Bol-Vermeer, Senior Shopper Activation Manager at CCEP Netherlands, puts it: “We entered the partnership with CuliQuiz because we both believe in building the hospitality of tomorrow. By sharing knowledge in a fun and accessible way, we’re making working in hospitality more enjoyable together.”

Great to start this partnership together.

Written by Tjitze van der Dam
