The building blocks

Four ingredients that work every day.

01

3-minute microlearning

Short. Playable. Contextual.

3 minutes a day, playable on the phone, fits in waiting time or a break, builds knowledge without training days. Addictively fun.

02

Themes & team battles

Short sprints, focused topics.

Short, targeted learning sprints around specific topics and soft skills. Competitions between departments with leaderboards to strengthen engagement and joy of play.

03

Game content is a mix of knowledge, behaviour & fun. Knowledge on the menu and upsell opportunities, combined with content on real situations. Recognisable and directly applicable.

200+ touchpoints per year

Always-on learning, always-on measurement.

More engagement, stronger product knowledge and more service confidence. All measurable, so you have clarity on quality per employee and per location.
