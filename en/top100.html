Thanks for your nomination!

We’ve received your submission. The expert panel reviews all nominations carefully.

Who can you nominate?

Anyone active in or for the hospitality sector is eligible. Think of:

  • Hospitality professionals
  • Teachers and practice supervisors
  • Foodservice or hospitality entrepreneurs
  • Suppliers or coaches
  • Bartenders, sommeliers or front-of-house

It’s not about fame, but about impact. Someone who really makes a difference.

What are we looking for?

An independent expert panel reviews nominations based on:

  • Contribution to the development of (young) professionals
  • Inspirational power and commitment to the craft
  • How someone brings others along or connects them
  • Practical examples that show what makes this person special

Note: it’s about what someone means for the craft right now. Not about a career title or the past.

What happens to your nomination?

If your candidate is selected:

  • They will appear in the CuliQuiz Top 100 of 2026
  • They’ll be invited to the announcement on Monday 21 September at Gastvrij Rotterdam
  • They’ll have a chance at one of three special awards: Education, Champion or Motivation Award

How does it work?

  1. Fill out the nomination form above.
  2. Briefly explain why this person is a champion.
  3. Add (optional) an example or quote from this person.
  4. You can nominate more than one person, but one submission per person.
